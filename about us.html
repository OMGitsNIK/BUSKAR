MAKING TRAVEL EASY FOR EVERYONE

Buses are one of the most used transport facilities across the country. We at Bus-kar aim to make travelling by bus a more convenient and a time efficient journey for all.

The problem:

In India, buses are the most popular means of public transportation but the route, traffic, mechanical issues, and other factors can all have an impact on when and where a bus arrives. People must wait at the bus station for a long period of time without knowing when the bus will arrive.There is currently no system in place that informs passengers if a bus is on schedule or not.

Our Idea:

To help reduce these problems we are here with this app which will do exactly what the commuters wish for.Our software allows commuters to track the real-time position of buses as well as bus e-tickets, minimising the burden of carrying change or standing in long lines. With this data at hand users can plan their trips and book tickets conveniently.
